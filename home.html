      Our Love story

      Sept, 2012

      First Meeting

      Andrew and Kathryn met at a Memorial Day BBQ co-sponsored by the Holy Name Young Adults Group, of which they were both members. They hit things off by having a great conversation about attending Montessori schools as children.

      Jul, 2014

      Dating

      The two stayed friends throughout the years. After one evening when Andrew and Kathryn were out together as part of a larger group, Andrew took the opportunity to ask Kathryn out again on her own for a date.

      Feb, 2015

      The Proposal

      Andrew and Kathryn attended a special Valentine's Day mass at Holy Name Cathedral preformed by Monsignor Mayall (their wedding celebrant). They went back to Andrew's house to grab some gifts before their dinner reservations. After Andrew gave Kathryn flowers and chocolates, he had one more extra special gift for Kathryn: a beautiful ring. He got down on one knee and proposed. It was perfect and she said yes!

      August, 2015

      Wedding Bells

      The couple will begin their lives together as husband and wife following a ceremony at their local parish church, Holy Name Cathedral, celebrated by Monsignor Mayall.
